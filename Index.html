The Ballwin
Treasure Hunt

A Quest of Five Legendary Locations

✦ ✦ ✦

Hearken, brave adventurer! The ancient treasures of Ballwin have been scattered across the land by the mischievous Spirit of Parkwatch. Five mighty artifacts now lie hidden in plain sight, waiting for a soul bold enough to seek them out.

Armed with only your wits and this most sacred map, you must journey from the great wilderness parks to the hallowed halls of history — each destination revealing a piece of the Grand Mystery of the West County.

⚓ Rules of the Quest

  1. Visit each location in order — the path must not be broken.
  2. Read the scroll at each destination carefully.
  3. Complete the challenge to unlock the next location.
  4. Collect all five seals to claim the final treasure!
